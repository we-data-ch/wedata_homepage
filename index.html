About us

We are an independent association dedicated to sharing our passion for data science and computer science, with a strong focus on statistics and computational methods. While our initial audience was people in the social sciences, we have since expanded our reach to various research fields and professional communities.



Concretely, the association pursues its goals by creating freely accessible educational content on multiple platforms, including YouTube videos, blog posts, exercises, and more. We also organize and offer consulting services, workshops, and talks to help individuals and organizations enhance their skills in data analysis and programming. Additionally, the association hosts regular events and presentations on relevant topics, aiming to foster knowledge sharing and community engagement in the digital and data science spheres
